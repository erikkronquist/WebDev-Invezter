INVEZTER

Use Invezter to buy and sell stocks. Not only can you buy and sell stocks, you can also see how the stock is doing and see how much you would have had if you invested earlier!

Intuitive Design

Invezter makes use of simple and smart designs to help you easily navigate the pages and find exactly what you are looking for. From your stock details to your transactions, it's all at your fingertips.

Deep Detail

Not only can you find what you are looking for, you can find detailed information about your stocks and shares, a graph so you can check how a stock is doing compared to days or months passed. The sector of the stock and company details are also available for you to dive into.
